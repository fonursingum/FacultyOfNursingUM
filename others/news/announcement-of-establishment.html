Announcement — Establishment of the Faculty of Nursing.

Universiti Malaya takes a significant step forward in strengthening education and the development of nursing.

Effective 6 March 2026, Universiti Malaya has officially established the Fakulti Kejururawatan — the Faculty of Nursing. The Faculty represents a decisive step in strengthening nursing academia in Malaysia and elevating the profession through dedicated academic and clinical training.

The Faculty was created as a strategic response to the country's shortage of nursing professionals, and to build a distinct academic home where the science, art and practice of nursing can flourish. It follows approvals by the University Senate (26 February 2026) and the Board of Directors (5 March 2026).

What the Faculty stands for

  • Vision — A global center of excellence in nursing scholarship, impacting healthcare delivery and society.
  • Mission — To advance the art and science of nursing by nurturing visionary leaders and clinical experts through transformative education, impactful research and clinical excellence.
  • Promise — Caring today, leading tomorrow.

What comes next

  • Appointment of the founding dean and management team (in progress)
  • Recruitment of academic and clinical staff across two founding departments
  • Curriculum development for the Bachelor of Nursing Science and postgraduate programmes
  • Clinical partnerships with UM Medical Centre and teaching hospitals
