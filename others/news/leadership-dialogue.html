Leadership Dialogue for the Dean Selection Exercise.

Together, we shape the future of nursing excellence.

On 6 July 2026, the Faculty of Nursing convened a Leadership Dialogue for the Dean Selection Exercise at the Faculty Meeting Room, Faculty of Medicine, Universiti Malaya. A meaningful platform for our esteemed candidates to share their vision, ideas and leadership aspirations for the future of the Faculty of Nursing.

The session was moderated by Professor Ir. Dr. Abdul Aziz bin Abdul Raman, Registrar (Pendaftar) of Universiti Malaya. Themed "Dialogue. Inspire. Lead.", it brought together senior academics, clinical leaders and members of the founding faculty community for candid conversation on nursing education, research direction and clinical impact.

"Together, let us continue to nurture leadership, drive innovation and make a difference in healthcare."

Highlights

  • Vision statements from each dean candidate on the faculty's five-year direction
  • Discussion on curriculum design for the founding Bachelor of Nursing Science cohort
  • Priorities for research and clinical partnerships across UM Medical Centre
  • Community, wellbeing and student-support pillars for the new faculty

Caring today, leading tomorrow.
