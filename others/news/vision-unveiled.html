Faculty of Nursing unveils its Vision.

A global center of excellence in nursing scholarship, impacting healthcare delivery and society.

Alongside its founding brand identity, the Faculty of Nursing unveiled its Vision — a compact statement of aspiration that will guide the years ahead:

"A global center of excellence in nursing scholarship, impacting healthcare delivery and society."

Three ideas are packed into that sentence. Global center of excellence — the Faculty benchmarks itself against the best nursing schools worldwide, and aspires to sit among them. Nursing scholarship — the Faculty is a place of active, rigorous scholarship as well as practice, spanning research, education and service. Impact on healthcare delivery and society — the work of the Faculty is meant to change how care is delivered, and how communities live and heal.

Alongside the vision

The Faculty also unveiled its Mission — a longer statement describing how it intends to pursue the vision: "To advance the art and science of nursing by nurturing visionary leaders and clinical experts through transformative education, impactful research, and clinical excellence in a diverse and dynamic global environment."
