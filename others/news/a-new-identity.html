A new identity — a stronger future.

Introducing the Faculty's visual identity, unifying the promise of care and scholarship under one banner.

With its academic status secured, the Faculty of Nursing turned to identity. On 15 March 2026, the Faculty unveiled its visual system — logo lockup, colour palette and typography — sitting inside the wider Universiti Malaya brand while carrying its own distinct voice.

The identity draws on the university's deep marine blue paired with a warmer gold accent, a combination that reads as trustworthy, established and welcoming. Typography combines a classical serif with a clean modern sans, echoing the balance between the enduring art of nursing and its evolving science.

Design principles

  • Rooted — anchored in the Universiti Malaya brand and its 60+ year academic history
  • Human — warmth, care and presence at the heart of every touchpoint
  • Forward-looking — a modern voice for a modern faculty preparing tomorrow's nurses

The new identity is applied across the Faculty's website, official communications and social channels — a visible sign of a growing community united behind a shared promise.
