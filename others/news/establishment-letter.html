9 March 2026

Official establishment letter issued.

The Registrar's office issues formal notification of the Faculty's establishment.

On 9 March 2026, Professor Ir. Dr. Abdul Aziz bin Abdul Raman, Registrar (Pendaftar) of Universiti Malaya, issued the formal notification (Pemakluman Penubuhan Fakulti Kejururawatan) announcing the establishment of the Faculty of Nursing to the university community.

The letter confirms the approvals that preceded the Faculty's founding — the Senate meeting of 26 February 2026 approving the proposal, and the Board of Directors (Lembaga Pengarah Universiti) meeting of 5 March 2026 endorsing that decision. The Faculty's establishment took effect on 6 March 2026.

The Faculty's official names shall be: Fakulti Kejururawatan (Bahasa Malaysia) and Faculty of Nursing (English).

The letter

The original notification, addressed to the university community:

Pemakluman Penubuhan Fakulti Kejururawatan · 9 March 2026

Signed by the Registrar and dated 09.03.2026, the letter marks the formal conclusion of the establishment process and the beginning of the Faculty's founding chapter.
