Community & Mental Health Nursing.

Public health, primary care, mental-health nursing and community wellbeing across the life course.

About the department

The Department of Community & Mental Health Nursing focuses on health promotion, disease prevention, primary care and mental-health nursing across the life course. We work with families, communities and health services to deliver research and teaching that improve population health and mental wellbeing.

Teaching focus

  • Community & Public Health Nursing (undergraduate core)
  • Mental Health Nursing (undergraduate core)
  • Maternal-Child & Family Nursing
  • Health Promotion and Behaviour Change
  • Global Health & Cultural Competence

Research themes

  • Community-based mental-health interventions
  • Non-communicable disease prevention and self-management
  • Maternal, adolescent and older-adult health
  • Digital health and community outreach
  • Social determinants of health in Malaysia

Community partners

Placements span primary-care clinics (Klinik Kesihatan), community mental-health services, non-governmental organisations and school health programmes across Kuala Lumpur and Selangor.
