Our Inception

A milestone for
Malaysian nursing.

Officially established by the Universiti Malaya Senate and Board of Directors on 6 March 2026, the Fakulti Kejururawatan represents a decisive strengthening of nursing academia in Malaysia.

Created as a strategic response to the national shortage of nurses, the Faculty is dedicated to world-class education, translational research and clinical excellence.

"The establishment of the Faculty of Nursing at Universiti Malaya is a strategic step to strengthen the nursing profession and produce highly-skilled, compassionate and future-ready nursing leaders for the nation."
— Prof. Dr. Hasniza Hashim, Vice-Chancellor
