Bachelor's

Bachelor of Nursing Science (Hons)

Our flagship undergraduate degree equips students with the clinical judgement, communication and leadership skills required of a modern registered nurse. Learning blends classroom instruction, simulation-based practice and real-world clinical placements from the very first year of study.

  • Anatomy, physiology and pathophysiology foundations
  • Medical-surgical, community, mental-health and maternal-child nursing
  • Simulation and objective structured clinical examinations (OSCEs)
  • Research methods and evidence-based practice
  • Final-year internship rotations across partner clinical sites

Programme structure

A four-year journey.

Y1

Foundations

Human sciences, nursing fundamentals, first clinical exposure.

Y2

Adult & Medical-Surgical

Medical-surgical nursing, pharmacology, simulation labs.

Y3

Specialty rotations

Community, mental-health, maternal-child, research project.

Y4

Internship & leadership

Extended clinical internship, leadership elective, capstone.

Entry requirements

  • STPM / Matriculation with credits in Biology, Chemistry and one additional science subject
  • SPM credits in Bahasa Malaysia, English, Mathematics and Science
  • MUET Band 4 or equivalent (English proficiency)
  • Interview and medical fitness screening
