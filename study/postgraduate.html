Master of Nursing Science (Coursework)

The programme "is designed to provide opportunities for nurses to develop advanced skills in professional nursing." Graduates acquire a rigorous grounding in research methods, critical analysis and theoretical perspectives that inform clinical nursing, together with competency in research design, critical appraisal and informed decision-making for practice improvement.

Structured around 42 credits — 27 core, 6 elective and 9 research — it balances taught modules with a research project of the student's choosing.

Who can apply.

Academic

CGPA 2.50–2.99 (at least one required)

  • Universiti Malaya graduate
  • Minimum 1 year related work experience
  • Publications in a related field

  • Faculty interview clearance

Specific qualifications

  • Bachelor's Degree in Nursing Science, Science (Nursing), Nursing or Health Sciences (Nursing)
  • Malaysian nurses must be registered with the Malaysian Nursing Board and hold a current practice licence
  • Post-basic qualification (≥ 6 months) or ≥ 1 year related field experience

English proficiency

One of the following is required:

  • MUET Band 4.0
  • IELTS (Academic) Band 6.0
  • TOEFL iBT (Centre-Based) 60
