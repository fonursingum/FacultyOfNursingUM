Our history.

From a Senate proposal to Malaysia's newest nursing faculty — the founding chapter.

The Faculty of Nursing (Fakulti Kejururawatan) was formally established at Universiti Malaya on 6 March 2026 — the culmination of a coordinated proposal championed by university leadership and endorsed by both the Senate and the Board of Directors (Lembaga Pengarah Universiti, LPU).

The Faculty was created as a strategic, dedicated initiative to address Malaysia's critical shortage of nurses and to elevate the profession through rigorous academic and clinical training. The establishment marked a significant milestone in strengthening nursing academic development in the country.
