About the Faculty.

A new academic home for Malaysia's nurses — dedicated to teaching, research and clinical excellence.

Who we are

Malaysia's newest
nursing faculty.

The Faculty of Nursing (Fakulti Kejururawatan) is Universiti Malaya's dedicated academic home for the science, art and practice of nursing. Officially established on 6 March 2026, we champion transformative education, impactful research and clinical excellence — with a mission to elevate the profession and strengthen the nation's healthcare system.

Guided by our promise "Caring today, leading tomorrow," we nurture visionary leaders and skilled clinicians for a diverse and dynamic global healthcare environment.
