Our purpose, our aspiration and our commitment to the future of nursing.

Our Aspiration

Vision

A global center of excellence in nursing scholarship, impacting healthcare delivery and society.

Our Commitment

Mission

To advance the art and science of nursing by nurturing visionary leaders and clinical experts through transformative education, impactful research, and clinical excellence in a diverse and dynamic global environment.

Guiding Principles

Our core values.

The values that anchor every classroom, laboratory and clinical encounter.

Compassion

Person-centred care as the heart of everything we teach and practise.

Scholarship

Rigorous evidence, curious minds and life-long learning.

Integrity

Professional ethics, accountability and respect for every patient.

Impact

Research and practice that improves lives, communities and systems.

Our Promise

Caring today,
leading tomorrow.
